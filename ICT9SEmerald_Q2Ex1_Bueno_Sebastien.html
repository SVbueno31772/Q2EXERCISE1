Dead memes

David Welch

The masters of mad mountains, the dark dwellers of deep,

the misty myth of memory slips into soulless sleep.

The timeless, tangled tree-tops, the perfect placid plains,


the eyes of heirs eternal, now bore the barraged brain.

Amidst the active androids, sharp, scintillating screens,

the lore of lasting legends just melds to mindless meme.
